Turkey Chili Recipe

Ingredients

  • Yellow Onions- finely chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, stemmed, seeded, and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 tablespoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt divided
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 pounds of ground turkey (85%-90% lean)
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans of dark kidney beans, drained and rinse
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of tomatoe puree
  • 1/2 cup of sweet and spicy BBQ sauce

Steps to Make Turkey Chili

  1. Put the Turkey in a pot and let it cook
  2. As the turkey cooks, wash and chop the pepper and onions
  3. When the turkey is completley cooked, add the cans of tomatoe puree and diced tomatoes
  4. Mix all the indrediats in the pot well
  5. Add the remaining ingediants to te pot and stir it all together
  6. Cover and cook on medium low for 25-30 minutes
  7. Serve on plate and enjoy
